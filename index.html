Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026 · Applies to every ch.roethlisberger.* app.

One short policy for every app I publish on Google Play — they all share the same minimal data practices.

What I collect

Directly: nothing. No servers, no accounts, no analytics, no login. Some apps process data only on your device — GPS, sensor, microphone or camera input is used live and then discarded, and never leaves your phone unless you choose to share it.

Third parties

Google AdMob shows one banner ad per app and may receive your advertising ID, coarse IP-based location, and device, OS and app version, governed by Google's privacy policy. You can reset the advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads in your device settings.

A few apps can optionally call Google's Gemini API, but only if you enter your own API key. Receipt Categorizer also uses on-device Google ML Kit text recognition (nothing is uploaded). The Google Play in-app review prompt is handled entirely by Google.

What I never do

No tracking, no profiling, no fingerprinting, no cookies, no selling of data, and no analytics SDKs such as Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics.

Permissions

Each permission maps to a feature you use: location for coordinates, heading and speed; camera for on-device receipt scanning; microphone for the sound meter; NFC to read tags you tap; vibration for haptic feedback; and internet plus the advertising ID for the banner ad.

Your data and rights

Anything an app stores — waypoints, history, API keys — stays on your device and is removed when you uninstall it or clear its data. Under GDPR, CCPA and similar laws you may request access or deletion, but since no data about you exists on my side, there is nothing to retrieve. These apps are not directed at children.

Data retention

I run no servers, so none of your personal data is retained on my side. Any data an app creates on your device — routes, waypoints, history, settings and API keys — is retained only on that device, for as long as you keep it, and is permanently deleted when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app. Data processed by Google AdMob for the banner ad is retained by Google in line with Google's Privacy Policy; you can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads in your device settings at any time.
